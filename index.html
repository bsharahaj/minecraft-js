The Three Tools

Axe

Chops down Tree & Leaf blocks.

Pickaxe

Mines Rock & Ore blocks.

Shovel

Digs Dirt & Sand blocks.

How to Play

  1. Pick a tool from the left dock — it lights up.
  2. Click a matching block in the world to break it.
  3. Broken blocks burst into your inventory (the chest).
  4. Open the chest, pick a block, then click an empty spot to place it.
  5. Hit ↻ Reset anytime to regenerate the world.
