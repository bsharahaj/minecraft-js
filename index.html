The Three Tools

🪓 Axe

Chops down Tree tiles.

⛏️ Pickaxe

Mines Rock tiles.

🥄 Shovel

Digs Dirt tiles.

How to Play

  1. Click a tool in the toolbar — it lights up.
  2. Click a tile in the world.
  3. If the tool matches the tile, the tile is removed.
  4. Removed tiles go into your inventory.
  5. Click an inventory tile, then an empty spot, to place it back.
